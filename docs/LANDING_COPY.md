## THE PROBLEM (3 columns)

**One model for everything**
Every task — trivial or hard — hits the same expensive model. You overpay for the
easy 80%.

**Vendor lock-in**
You're stuck with one lab's model and its rate limits, outages, and price hikes.

**No memory, no learning**
Your assistant forgets everything between sessions and never gets better at *your*
work.

---

## THE SOLUTION — how [PRODUCT NAME] works (4 steps)

1. **Assess** — Sarva scores its own confidence on your task.
2. **Route** — easy tasks run on a cheap/free model; hard ones go to the best
   teacher for the job.
3. **Refine** — delegated answers are verified and corrected before they reach you.
4. **Learn** — every task makes the router smarter, so more work stays cheap over
   time.

> Security is built into the loop: a permission gate sits between every decision
> and every action, with a credential vault and an injection firewall — not
> bolted on afterward.
